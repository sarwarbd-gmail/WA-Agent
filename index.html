Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how WA Agent ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you interact with our business through WhatsApp, including our automated messaging assistant.

1. Information We Collect

When you message us on WhatsApp, we may collect:

  • Your WhatsApp phone number
  • The content of the messages you send us
  • Basic interaction data, such as message timestamps and which topics you asked about

We do not collect payment information, precise location data, or any information beyond what you voluntarily share with us in conversation.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you share to:

  • Respond to your questions about our products, pricing, and orders
  • Provide automated replies to frequently asked questions
  • Connect you with a human team member when needed
  • Improve our responses and product offerings over time

3. How We Store and Protect Your Information

Message data and conversation state (such as how many times you've messaged us recently) are stored securely. We take reasonable measures to protect this information from unauthorized access, but no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

Our WhatsApp messaging is powered by Meta's WhatsApp Business Platform (Cloud API). When you message us, your information is also subject to WhatsApp's own Privacy Policy, as Meta processes messages to deliver them between you and our business account.

5. Data Retention

We retain conversation data only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiries and improve our service. Session/tracking data used for automated replies.

7. Children's Privacy

Our WhatsApp service is intended for individuals who are legally able to make purchasing decisions in Bangladesh. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us
